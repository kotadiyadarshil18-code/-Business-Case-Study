Q1 Which region is underperforming and why?

The South region shows lower completed sales and a higher number of cancelled orders compared to other regions. This may indicate customer dissatisfaction or weaker sales performance in that area.

Q2 Are certain agents associated with more cancellations?

Yes, some sales agents have a higher number of cancelled orders. This could be due to poor customer handling, delayed responses, or ineffective sales communication.

Q3 Which category needs promotion or improvement?

The Accessories category has lower total sales compared to Electronics and Furniture. This category may need better marketing strategies and promotional offers to improve performance.

Q4 Is customer dissatisfaction concentrated in a region?

Yes, customer dissatisfaction appears to be higher in regions with more cancelled orders. This suggests that service quality or delivery issues may be affecting customer experience in those regions.
